Account Deletion Request

Civilify - UPSC Exam Preparation

We respect your privacy and your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your Civilify account and associated data, please follow the instructions below.

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data, including topics, quiz history, and progress, will be permanently removed.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to hellocivilify@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your registered email address in the email body
  4. We will process your request within 48 hours
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete
Send Deletion Request

What Data Will Be Deleted

The following data will be permanently deleted:

  • Your account profile (name, email, phone number)
  • All topics created by you
  • Quiz history and results
  • Progress tracking data
  • App preferences and settings

Data Retention Period

Once we receive your deletion request, we will delete your data within a reasonable timeframe, typically within 30 days. Some data may be retained for a limited period to comply with legal obligations or for legitimate business purposes.

Alternative Options

If you only want to temporarily stop using the app without deleting your account, you can simply uninstall the app. Your data will remain stored and you can reinstall the app later to continue using your account.
